what is sobremesa?

Sobremesa is an exploratory and speculative design project that aims to create reflection on the way we connect to food in our past, present, and future. In Spanish, sobremesa translates to “upon the table” and it is the Hispanic tradition of relaxing at the table after a heavy meal. The table represents a centerpiece for connections and reflections through conversations. I want to create an experience that allows us to digest not only food but our thoughts.
